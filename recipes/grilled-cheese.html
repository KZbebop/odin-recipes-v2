Grilled Cheese

Description

Easy and delicious snack! It can be done in less than 5 minutes or less.

Ingredients

  • Unsalted butter
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • 2 Bread slices

Steps

  1. Smear the outer sides of the bread slices with butter
  2. Add cheddar cheese to the bread slices
  3. Fold it into a sandwich and then start toasting each side on a pan that's on medium heat
  4. Once the buttered sides are golden brown and the cheese inside is melted, the sandwich is done
