Scrambled Eggs

Description

Easy and quick breakfast meal that can be done under 5 minutes!

Ingredients

  • Big knob of butter
  • 3 eggs

Steps

  1. Crack the 3 eggs into a bowl and then whisk until the whites and yolks are mixed into each other
  2. Add the knob of butter into the pan on medium heat, once it's melted and bubbling, add the egg mixture
  3. While cooking, use a spatula to flip the bottom part of the scrambled egg to the top for even cooking
  4. Note that this will be a quick process so pay attention to the texture as it cooks, you want to achieve a texture that isn't too runny nor rubbery
  5. Transfer into your dish, salt to taste and enjoy!
