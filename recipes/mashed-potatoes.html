Mashed Potatoes

Description

Excellent dish! Very filling and goes with plenty of savory dishes.

Ingredients

  • 6 Potatoes
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2 cup salted butter
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Peel and cut potatoes into chunks
  2. Add them into a pot with enough cold water to cover
  3. Set it on high heat and let it boil until the potatoes are fork tender
  4. Drain the potatoes to a collander
  5. Return them to the pot and then add the whipping cream and butter
  6. Using a potato masher or a large serving spoon, mash the potatoes until the desired texture is achieved. It must be smooth and creamy but not runny
  7. Salt to taste
